What happens during the mobile digitization process?

+

AVACompli deploys specialized teams to your facilities worldwide to digitize decades of paper compliance records:

Step 1: Site Assessment

  • Our team surveys your archive storage locations
  • Estimates volume and categorizes document types
  • Plans logistics for on-site scanning operations

Step 2: Secure Setup

  • Temporary scanning facilities established at your offices
  • Enterprise-grade OCR scanners deployed
  • Chain of custody procedures implemented
  • Background-checked personnel assigned

Step 3: Digitization

  • Documents scanned at 300+ DPI for optimal OCR accuracy
  • Automatic categorization and tagging
  • Quality assurance checks on every batch
  • Secure encrypted upload to cloud storage

Step 4: AI Indexing

  • Machine learning analyzes document content
  • Automatic classification by type, date, relevance
  • Full-text search capability in 100+ languages
  • Metadata extraction and organization

Timeline: 40-year archives become fully searchable in approximately 8 weeks per location.

Security: All digitized documents maintain audit trails, access controls, and chain of custody documentation required for regulated industries.
